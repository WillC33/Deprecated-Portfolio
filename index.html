William Cooke

I am a passionate full-stack developer, with a fascination for Agile processes. In my current role, I work on maintaining and developing projects in ASP.NET web API, React, and even legacy code in WinForms, giving me knowledge of a wide range of software paradigms and approaches.

Before I began developing, I ran my own communication coaching practice for several years and though the work was satisfying, it was always the technical aspects of the operation that kept me feeling challenged and engaged.

Working with communication gave me an interest in the presentation and movement of information and data within software and a particular passion for the development and consumption of APIs.

My various projects in work have allowed me knowledge of managing modern enterprise software through VCS, CI/CD, and refactoring to account for modern best practices. My personal projects range from WordPress websites to browser extensions, and can be seen below.

Though still early in my career I have a deep love of development and a drive to continue learning.

Furthermore, I am a skilled remote worker. I have lived and worked in 11 countries. I speak several languages to varying degrees of fluency and have highly developed communication skills. I am experiencing so many growth opportunities, and I intend to push myself to learn and use my highly developed career and soft skills to help me.
